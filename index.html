Our Story

From Heartbreak to Bake

Gracie

My sister and I are the sad girl bakers. The sad girl club is a club you don’t really want to be in, but you’ll probably hold the membership card at least once in your life. As I write this, I am what the movies call broken hearted. Life has its ‘moments’, but when they strike, we all have our thing that gets us through it. And if your thing is freshly baked goods. You’re in the right place. We want to provide the bakes that get you through hard times. That’s it. It’s that simple.

Alice

My story is a little different to my sisters, as I found comfort in the baked good itself. Some may say, “Great job starting a habit of emotionally eating” but for me, when Gracie would whip something up it was just a big “I’m sorry you’re going through something sucky but here’s a brownie” and just that little gesture would really cheer me up, even if I was still eating through tears. So that’s what we want to do, provide that little gesture that proves that the world isn’t ending.
