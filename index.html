Our Story

From Heartbreak to Bake

Alice

My story will probably be rather different to my sisters, as instead of finding comfort in the actual baking process I just found the comfort in the baked good itself. Some may say “oh great job Al on starting a habit of emotionally eating” but for me when I’ve been heartbroken it really does feel like your whole world has caved in and eating is probably the last thing I wanted to do, so when Grace would whip something up in the kitchen it was just a big sign to me that said ‘I’m sorry you’re going through something sucky but here’s a brownie’ and just that little gesture would really cheer me up (even if I might of eaten through tears still) and that’s what we wanna do to with Sad Girl Bakers, cheer your bestie up, support the people you love, buy a cake yourself if that’s what you need right now! Sending all the good vibes from another broken hearted baker.

Love, Al x
